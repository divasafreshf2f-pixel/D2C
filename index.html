About Divasa Fresh

Who We Are

Divasa Fresh is an agricultural aggregation company designed to simplify and strengthen the connection between farmers and markets.

We work directly with farmers as long-term partners, aggregate their produce, ensure quality standards, and distribute to reliable buyers—creating value for every stakeholder in the system.

What We Do

  • Partner with farmers across local regions
  • Aggregate fruits and vegetables at scale
  • Ensure quality, grading, and consistency
  • Supply to households, supermarkets, hotels, and institutions

We focus on efficiency, fairness, and transparency—not unnecessary middle layers.

Why Divasa Fresh Exists

In the traditional supply chain, farmers face uncertain demand and pricing, buyers face inconsistent quality, and consumers lack transparency.

Divasa Fresh exists to organize this ecosystem by acting as a responsible aggregator that balances farmer welfare with market needs.

Mission & Vision

Mission:
To empower farmers through fair aggregation, deliver consistent quality to buyers, and build a transparent agricultural supply network.

Vision:
To become a trusted aggregation platform that improves farmer income stability and strengthens India’s fresh produce supply chain.

How Divasa Fresh Works

Farmer Partners → Divasa Fresh (Aggregation & Quality Control) → Organized Distribution → Consumers | Supermarkets | Hotels | Institutions

Divasa Fresh acts as the central aggregation and coordination layer. We consolidate produce from farmer partners, ensure quality consistency, manage logistics, and supply reliable volumes to buyers—creating efficiency, fairness, and transparency across the supply chain.

Farmers Are Our Partners

At Divasa Fresh, farmers are partners in growth—not just suppliers.

  • Transparent pricing mechanisms
  • Aggregation that improves bargaining power
  • Reduced market uncertainty
  • Faster movement of produce to market
  • Long-term collaboration

When farmers grow sustainably, the entire ecosystem benefits.
