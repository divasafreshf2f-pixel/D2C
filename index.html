Building a Fair, Transparent Farm-to-Market Food System

Divasa Fresh is a professional farm-to-market fresh produce platform connecting Indian farmers directly with households and businesses through structured sourcing, quality discipline, and reliable supply operations.

About Divasa Fresh

Divasa Fresh – Farmers to Family was created with a clear purpose: to build a responsible, efficient, and transparent farm-to-market system that works for farmers, consumers, and professional buyers alike.

India’s agricultural supply chain is complex and fragmented. Farmers often operate without demand visibility, while buyers face inconsistency in quality and reliability. Divasa Fresh bridges this gap by acting as a structured aggregation and distribution platform rather than a traditional trading intermediary.

Our focus is not on short-term transactions, but on building long-term sourcing relationships, operational discipline, and trust across the value chain. Every decision we take is guided by sustainability, quality, and fairness.

Vision & Mission

“When farmers grow, the nation grows.”

Our vision is to create a sustainable food ecosystem where farmers earn fairly, food quality is respected, and consumers receive honest produce through transparent systems.

Our mission is to design and operate a disciplined farm-to-market platform that supports predictable sourcing, professional quality standards, and dependable delivery across households and businesses.

Our Farm-to-Market Model

Farm → Aggregation → Quality Check → Distribution → Customer

Divasa Fresh operates through a clearly defined sourcing and distribution model designed to maintain freshness, consistency, and accountability at every stage.

Produce is sourced directly from farmers and farmer groups, aggregated through organized collection points, quality-verified, and distributed efficiently to customers without unnecessary storage cycles.

Built for HORECA & Professional Buyers

Professional kitchens require reliability, consistency, and quality discipline. Divasa Fresh supports hotels, restaurants, cafés, cloud kitchens, and institutional buyers through structured procurement and dependable delivery.

Our systems are designed to align with professional kitchen standards, ensuring produce meets usage requirements, grading expectations, and operational timelines.
